SEVENTEEN/ SVT (세븐틴) is a South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment. They consist of S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Woozi, DK, Mingyu, The8, Seungkwan, Vernon, and Dino. Seventeen is also divided into 3 units: Vocal Team, Hip Hop Team, and Performance Team. The name Seventeen can also be interpreted as follows : 13 members + 3 sub-units + 1 group = 17 (Seventeen). The group has achieved commercial success and boasts a large fanbase known as "Carat"

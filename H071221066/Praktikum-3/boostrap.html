Profil Seventeen

Seventeen (세븐틴), also stylized as SEVENTEEN or SVT, is a South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment in 2015. The group consists of 13 members divided into three sub-units, each with a different area of specialization : a 'Hip-Hop Team', 'Vocal Team', and 'Performance Team'.
13 members + 3 sub-units + 1 group = 17 (SEVENTEEN)
